About Us

Budget Vacations is a veteran founded and operated business and a proud supporter of our nation's defenders, veterans and first responders.

Budget Vacations was started by a group of Service Members that were frustrated by the travel industries hidden fees and complicated processes.

The staff at Budget Vacations understands the mindset of every customer that needs to make travel plans. We are proud to go the extra distance to meet our customers needs. All of our travel packages are competitive in prices to other sites and will price match.

We donate 10% of our profits to the Wounded Warrior Project so we can give a little back to those that made the ultimate sacrafice.

  • 10% of our proceeds and profits go back to Veteran funded programs and charities
